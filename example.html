ABOUT US

Zenko is an established FMCG distribution company, and is one of the largest serving the Kenyan Retail market. From our central location in the Nairobi region – at Nyahera Road, Industrial Area Lunga Lunga – we import, warehouse and distribute to hypermarkets, supermarkets, mini-marts and kiosks across the country. Beginning in Eastliegh – one of Nairobi’s key business districts – as a general provision store over 30 years ago, we have since grown to become a leading distributor of consumer goods in Kenya. Our inspiration is to fulfill the trust placed in us by our clients, by the retailers and by the end-consumers.
